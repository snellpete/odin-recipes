Lasagna

Ingredients

  • Lasagna noodles
  • Marinara sauce
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Parmesean cheese

Directions

  1. Cook noodles per package instructions
  2. Drain noodles
  3. Make a layer of noodles in pan
  4. Top noodles with sauce
  5. Sprinkle with mozzarella cheese
  6. Repeat steps 2-4 with remaining ingredients
  7. Bake until golden, about 40 minutes
